4-Layer Threat Scanner

Filename Scanner

                    
Instantly verifies filename signatures against known clients (Wurst, Meteor, Sigma, Vape, Impact, Mathax, etc.) using regex rules.

Scans internal configurations, including fabric.mod.json, mods.toml, and MANIFEST.MF metadata for identity tags.
